Journey to the Top of
Tournament Fishing

My name is Mike Yeager and I am fishing the FLW Bass Fishing League as a boater. I have fished my whole life. I am very competitive by nature and love to fish, so tournament fishing is awesome. I am married to my best friend who supports my fishing addiction and we have five beautiful daughters.

Mike Knows His Gear

I have a Nitro Z9 with a 250 Mercury ProXS! I get a lot of people who ask me "how do you like your Nitro?" Well, I love it. If you are interested in one, just give me a shout and we will take it out and you can decide for yourself. Alan at Sundown Marine in Glenpool can fix you right up. They are excellent to work with.

I have recently joined the RPM Custom Rods Pro Staff and love their rods. My favorite is the Rattle Trap Special because I love to throw a crankbait.

I am also on the Mountaineer Jigs Pro Staff. They make awesome custom jigs. I also have Farmer's Insurance. I hope that I never have to use them but they are there if you need them. Nick at Farmer's can also fix you right up.
